RVTN Prime Repository

This site is the repository for RVTN Prime, the RVTN Public Key Infrastructure (PKI). The repository is publicly available at pki.rvtn.org.

The PKI includes a variety of Certification Authorities (CAs), which issue X.509 certificates for both public and RVTN Enterprise (non-public) use.

Certificates and Certificate Revocation Lists (CRLs), as well as other documents of interest, are available below.

RVTN Root Authority

The RVTN Root Authority is the primary trust anchor for the RVTN PKI.

Prime Root Certificate -- DER encoded binary (.crt)

Prime Root Certificate -- Base-64 encoded ASCII (.pem)

Prime Root CRL -- Published Annually

RVTN Prime Enterprise Authority

The RVTN Prime Enterprise Authority operates in accordance with the Prime Enterprise Certification Practices Statement (CPS).

This certification authority offers 4 levels of assurance as defined in the CPS: basic, low, medium, and high.

Please note that certificates issued by the RVTN Enterprise Authority are not intended for public use. Such certificates are marked 'Intended for RVTN Enterprise use only.'

Prime Enterprise Certificate (Valid after 2023-12-02) -- DER encoded binary (.crt)

Prime Enterprise Certificate (Valid after 2023-12-02) -- Base-64 encoded ASCII (.pem)

Prime Enterprise CRL (Valid after 2023-12-02) -- Published Annually

Prime Enterprise (Valid before 2024-04-03) -- DER encoded binary (.crt)

Prime Enterprise Certificate (Valid before 2024-04-03) -- Base-64 encoded ASCII (.pem)

Prime Enterprise CRL (Valid before 2024-04-03) -- Published Annually

RVTN Prime IE2 Authority

The RVTN Prime Enterprise Authority IE2 is an online issuing certification authority. This CA superceded the RVTN Prime IE1 Authority on 2023-12-03.

This certification authority offers 3 levels of assurance as defined in the Prime Enterprise CPS: basic, low, and medium.

Certificates from this authority are only issued for the following purposes: Server Authentication, Client Authentication, Secure Email, IPsec End System, IPsec tunnel termination, IPsec User, IPsec IKE Intermediate, KDC Authentication, Private Key Archival, Certificate Request Agent, and Remote Desktop Authentication.

Please note that certificates issued by the RVTN Enterprise Authority are not intended for public use. Such certificates are marked 'Intended for RVTN Enterprise use only.'

Prime Enterprise IE2 Certificate -- DER encoded binary (.crt)

Prime Enterprise IE2 Certificate -- Base-64 encoded ASCII (.pem)

Prime Enterprise IE2 CRL -- Published Weekly

RVTN Prime IE1 Authority (Valid before 2024-02-14)

The RVTN Prime Enterprise Authority IE1 was an online issuing certification authority.

This certification authority offered 3 levels of assurance as defined in the Prime Enterprise CPS: basic, low, and medium.

Certificates from this authority were only issued for the following purposes: Server Authentication, Client Authentication, Secure Email, IPsec End System, IPsec tunnel termination, IPsec User, IPsec IKE Intermediate, KDC Authentication, Private Key Archival, and Remote Desktop Authentication.

Please note that certificates issued by the RVTN Enterprise Authority are not intended for public use. Such certificates are marked 'Intended for RVTN Enterprise use only.'
